## The software process models

```bash
📁 The waterfall model. A plan-driven model. Separate and distinct phases of specificaiton and development.

📁 Incremental development. Specificaiton, development and validation are interleaved. May be plan driven or agile. Brings our human abilities thus does not work with a plan alone.

📁 Integration and configuration.

```

### Why its not used: Not Evolutionary and not Incremental
```bash
Evolutionary:
📍 Technology is always changing
📍 Customer requirements change
📍 Software cannot be built perfectly all at once
Incremental:
📍 Risk is reduced while building incrementally
```
